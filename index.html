CV

I'm a Dickson Instructor and NSF Postdoc at the University of Chicago.
Before that I was a graduate student at Princeton where my advisor was Fernando Coda Marques.
I will be on the tenure track job market in 2025.

My office is Eckhart 408B.

Email: loweb24 {at} uchicago.edu
Research interests: Differential Geometry, Minimal Surfaces, Hyperbolic Geometry, Dynamical Systems, Scalar Curvature, k-Surfaces, Locally Symmetric Spaces.
Much of my work is focused on rigidity phenomena, in a variety of different geometric situations.
Here are some surveys/notes that might be helpful if you want to learn more.
