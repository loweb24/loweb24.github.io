I’m a Dickson Instructor and NSF Postdoc at the University of Chicago. Before that I was a graduate student at Princeton where my advisor was Fernando Coda Marques. I will be on the tenure track job market in the fall of 2025.

Research Interests

Differential Geometry, Minimal Surfaces, Hyperbolic Geometry, Dynamical Systems, Scalar Curvature, k-Surfaces, Locally Symmetric Spaces. Much of my work is focused on rigidity phenomena, in a variety of different geometric settings.

Here are some surveys/notes that might be helpful if you want to learn more.
