I'm a Dickson Instructor and NSF Postdoc at the University of Chicago.

My office is Eckhart 408B.

Research interests: Differential Geometry, Minimal Surfaces, Hyperbolic Geometry, Dynamical Systems, Scalar Curvature, Locally Symmetric Spaces.
Much of my work is focused on rigidity phenomena in a variety of different geometric situations.
